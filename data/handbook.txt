Employee Vacation Policy
Effective April 2026  |  Human Resources
1. Purpose
This policy sets out how vacation time is earned, requested, and used across the organization. We believe that taking time away from work is essential to staying healthy, energized, and effective. This policy exists to make sure that everyone understands their entitlements and that the process for taking time off is fair and easy to navigate.

2. Eligibility
All full-time and part-time employees are covered by this policy from their first day of employment. Temporary, contract, and freelance workers are not eligible for company-paid vacation unless explicitly stated in their contract.

Part-time employees receive vacation on a pro-rata basis calculated from their contracted hours relative to a standard 40-hour week.

4. Carryover and Expiry
Unused vacation days may be carried over into the following calendar year, up to the limits shown in the table above. Any days exceeding the carryover cap will be forfeited on January 1st unless a carry-forward has been approved in writing by HR. We strongly encourage employees to take their full entitlement each year.

5. Requesting Vacation
To request vacation time, please follow the steps below:
Submit your request through the HR system at least two weeks in advance for requests of up to five consecutive days.
For requests of six or more consecutive days, please give at least four weeks' notice wherever possible.
Your manager will approve or decline your request within three business days.
Approval depends on business needs and team coverage. We will do our best to accommodate all requests.
Once approved, you will receive a confirmation email and your calendar will be updated automatically.
